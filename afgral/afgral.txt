# Afgral

-- slide 0 --

Afgral is a french association promoting the use and the development of free tools and content in the graphics and creative industry.

-- slide 1 --

Since 2011, Afgral has been organising workshops and trainings about

- Game design and art
- Illustrations
- Web development, marketing and esign
- Ebooks creation

-- slide 2 --

Afgral is also organizing a yearly GrafikLabor over a weekend with talks, workshops and hackatons.

They also coordinate the local Blender User Group in Rennes.
